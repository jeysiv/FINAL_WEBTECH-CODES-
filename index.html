About the Game

“Not A Trap” is a platformer game designed to trick players with unexpected obstacles, including falling floors, spikes, and other traps scattered across each level.

It is inspired by “Level Devil” by Unept and requires players to avoid both the visible and non-visible traps in order to reach the goal and advance to the next level.

Mechanics

MOVEMENTS:
- Left/Right arrow for left and right movement
- Space for jump movement
- Enter/Return for resetting the player


HOW TO PLAY:
1. Select "Play" and choose "Level 1" from the Start Menu.
2. Navigate through obstacles such as pits, spikes, and traps along your path.
3. Enter doors to progress to the next level.
4. Keep your death count within the maximum allowed limit per major level.
5. Complete all sublevels within a major level to unlock the next stage.
6. Use the EXIT, RESET, and PLAY buttons whenever necessary.
7. Enjoy the game and have fun!

Features

DEATH-LIMIT SYSTEM:
      - Requires a maximum number of deaths for each major level.
      - Exceeding the maximum number of deaths will not allow you to advance to the         next level.
      - The death counter adds up all your deaths from sublevels 1-3.


VISIBLE AND NON-VISIBLE TRAPS
      - Includes both obvious obstacles and hidden traps that activate in not
        the most predictable ways.


MENTAL ENDURANCE CHALLENGE
      - Tests the player's patience, reflexes, and adaptability in a trial-and-error       game style.
        - Challenges the player's focus and consistency while progressing through       increasingly difficult obstacles.
